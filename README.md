# 🚀 Enterprise CRM & CX SaaS Platform Simulator

A multi-platform, enterprise-grade CRM and Customer Experience (CX) simulator inspired by industry-leading platforms like Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics 365, SAP CRM, and Oracle CX.

This project demonstrates real-world CRM workflows, enterprise business processes, and customer analytics through a unified web-based interface.

---

## 🌐 Platforms Included

### ☁️ Salesforce Sales Cloud
- Lead Management
- Account & Contact Management
- Opportunity Tracking
- Sales Pipeline Visualization

### 🟦 Microsoft Dynamics 365
- Customer Management (Unified View)
- Business Operations & Sales
- Activity Tracking
- Insights Dashboard

### 🟥 SAP CRM
- Customer Master Data
- Sales & Distribution
- Service Management
- Business Process Flow Integration

### 🟧 Oracle CX
- Customer Experience Analytics
- Engagement Tracking
- Customer Journey Mapping
- AI-driven Insights

---

## 🧩 Key Features

- Multi-platform CRM ecosystem in a single application
- App Launcher & Platform Switcher
- Interactive dashboards with KPIs and charts
- ITIL-inspired workflows (Incident, Service, Change)
- Customer and sales lifecycle simulation
- Enterprise-style UI/UX design
- Modular navigation (Leads, Customers, Opportunities, Insights)
